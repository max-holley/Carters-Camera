Hello, I'm Tash

I am a passionate photographer who loves to be outdoors! I am a mum to two children, family shoots have always been a love of mine however with having my own children I now know what they are like! So rest assured I will get them to be relaxed and enjoy the photoshoot no matter what their personality is they are in good hands.

I am also married so I know how hectic the big day can be and the stress of wanting everything to be perfect, so don’t worry I’ll have your back for everything you could want, Need some water? there will be fresh bottles in my car! Need a snack ? Always a few in my bag! Need to have a quick look at any images to make sure you look ok? No problem just ask! Forgot something back at your room? Hand me the keys I will grab it for you! No need to stress! I will be more than a photographer capturing smiles and real moments, I’ll be a friend and a helpful hand!

And finally for my last specialised area is business photography, this is where I started my professional career and something that will forever be in my heart! I love getting to know about a business so I can try and capture the true vibe of the place, from a dark and romantic restaurant to a light and fun gym I’ve photographed it all! Just get in touch to see some of my work! It’s not just locations I’ve taken images for either, I’ve taken images for catalogs and headshots! Photography is my passion and what ever project I take on I will put my heart and soul into it.
