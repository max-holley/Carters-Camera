I offer a variety of packages for weddings so you can find one that fits you and your budget! I am a relaxed photographer, who will blend into the background, you will hardly notice me there! We will have at least 2 meetings before your wedding, the first will be so you can meet me and I can show you my portfolio. The next will be 3/4 weeks before your wedding where we will talk about exactly what you want from me by showing me some example images that you are thinking and seeing the timings of the day.
